💡 Interview Tips
01

Think Before Speaking

Take 5 seconds to structure. Use "First… Then… Finally…" framework.

02

Use Real Scenarios

"In a real project, you would…" shows practical understanding.

03

Know Automation

Flow Builder dominates. Know Before-Save vs After-Save, Scheduled Paths.

04

Security Model #1

Expect 3-5 questions on OWD, Role Hierarchy, Profiles, Sharing Rules.

05

Data Migration

Know Data Loader, Upsert, External IDs, parents before children.

06

Be Honest

"I understand the concept…" builds trust when you have gaps.

07

Ask Smart Questions

Ask about the team's SF edition or deployment process. Shows interest.

08

Mention Certs

Even "in progress" shows commitment to the platform.
